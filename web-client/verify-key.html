Key Verification

Cross-check a user's encryption key against the on-chain KeyRegistry transparency log on Ethereum Sepolia. This reads the blockchain directly from your browser — not through the mailbox server — and compares what the chain publicly records against what the server currently reports, so a server that substitutes a key is caught.

Users are identified on-chain by keccak256(username) (the “identity hash” below) rather than by an Ethereum address, since accounts do not hold wallets — the server’s registrar wallet posts registrations on their behalf.
